University of Queensland Tae Kwon Do Club

The University of Queensland Tae Kwon Do Club website is very media heavy with lots of videos and images, but can be hard to view on some screens and with some brightnesses due to the very dark theme. The formatted table with all the download links of the different patterns is very useful and UI friendly, however the red and black theme can be hard to read at times.

Tien Lung Taekwon-Do

The Tien Lung Taekwon-Do club has a very interactive website, that has lots of visuals and utilizes in-page jumps, and hover styles. However, the page is mostly contained on one single page, and can be a bit awkward to navigate, and find what you are looking for.

Abbotsford Taekwondo Club

I found the Abbotsford Taekwondo club to have a very un-intuitive website, lots of the images (Like the facebook/Instagram) aren't links but just images, as well as the top horizontal navigation list spills over onto the next line. The location page seems to be missing elements completely, and the Contact Us and Taekwondo Affiliation pages unexpectedly link to outside webpage, and mail links.


Free Components

openclipart

One of the best parts of openclipart is that their license allows for unlimited commercial use, and they contain a plethora of over 158,000 images for use that is always growing.

freesound

Although the sounds on the website are free, certain sounds contain usage restrictions for commercial use, however, you are bound to find a sound for whatever you are looking for from common sounds to the very unique.

Google Fonts

Google fonts allows use for commercial or personal with their open source license; they host very high quality fonts that you can integrate into your website, from the wacky to the most popular they host it all.
